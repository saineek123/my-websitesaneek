Deposit Limits

We offer deposit limits to help you manage your spending and promote responsible use of our services.

1. Setting Deposit Limits

You can set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits on your account to control how much you can deposit.

2. How to Change Limits

To set or change your deposit limits, go to your account settings or contact our support team for assistance.

3. Increasing Limits

Requests to increase deposit limits may require a waiting period to ensure responsible spending practices.

4. Need Help?

If you need help setting limits or managing your account, please contact our support team at: support@example.com

Security Activity

Your account security is our top priority. Here you can learn about monitoring and managing your security activity.

1. View Recent Activity

You can view your recent login history, devices used, and any changes made to your account settings.

2. Alerts & Notifications

We send security alerts if we detect suspicious login attempts or changes to your account. Please keep your contact details up to date.

3. What to Do if You See Suspicious Activity

  • Change your password immediately.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) if not already active.
  • Contact our support team if you notice any unauthorized activity.

4. Need Help?

If you have any security concerns, please reach out to us at: security@example.com
